Immortal Jellyfish

With the ability to reverse the biotic cycle, these jellyfish are the only known biologically immortal species.

  • Scientific Name: Turritopsis dohrnii
  • Average Length: 4.5 millimeters
  • Diet: plankton, fish eggs, and small mollusks
  • Habitat: Mediterranean Sea and waters of Japan

The immortal jellyfish is known for its ability to revert back to the polyp stage when exposed to environmental stress. However, in nature, individuals can still succumb to death by predation or disease in the medusa stage.
